Product Images In Order Lines

Product Images in List-View/Order-Lines

The module makes the product lines visually more helpful for the Odoo users by showing the product images next to the product listed in various forms such orderlines and delivery lines. The user can directly see the product image without opening the product.

Details Matter So We Added product Images In various views in Odoo

To make working with the product easier and visually helpful the module shows product Images in Various Views in Odoo.

  • Provides Convenience to the user.
  • Saves time and effort.
  • No need to open the product to see the product image. It is now shown next to the product in list view.
  • Approve/disapprove new product requests from sellers.
  • Gives the idea of product while adding the sales order or purchase order line.
Detailed Features List
Below is the detailed list of Feature for Product Images In Order Lines
Product Image in Product List View
  • The module adds the features of showing the product images in various forms in Odoo.
  • A separate column for product images is added to the product list view in Odoo.
  • The product image is also visible in list view in the product variants menu in Odoo.
Product Image in Sale Order Line
  • Moreover, the module shows the product image in the sale orderline when the product is added to it.
Product Image in Purchase Order line
  • When creating the purchase order, the product image is shown in the purchase orderline of the product added to it.
Product Image in Delivery Orderline
  • The delivery generated for the order also shows the product image(s) of the products in the delivery orderline.
SCREENSHOTS
  • New Product Image Column in the Odoo Products List View
  • Adds Product Image column in Odoo Product variants List view
  • Shows Product Image in Sales Orders Lines
  • Shows Product Images in Purchase Orders Lines
  • Also Shows Product Image in Delivery Orders Lines when delivery is generated
